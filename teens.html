At Jesus House Abby, we are not just concerned with how our teenagers feel, but also how they think. Which is why, we have a class devoted to teaching them sound biblical lessons alongside fun activities to keep them engaged.

2018 Lessons

This year (2018), the teenagers will be learning lessons from Jesus' parables. If you're interested in learning along, you can join us at Church or use the study outline provided below. We also meet once a month, in a more relaxed setting to go over some of the concepts learned in class over pizza, movies, games, etc.

Discipline & Dilligence

There is hope even when it seems like our labor is futile. We may not know how success will come, but we know what we need to do to achieve success.
Key Activity: Set Goals for the year
Key Passage: Mark 4: 26-29
Supporting Passages: 1 Corinthians 3:6, Hebrews 6:10-11, Galatians 6:7-10, Hebrews 12:11

Salvation

Our response to God's forgiveness is our Love, not the other way around.
Key Activity: Identify behaviors common among teens that do not express love for God
Key Passage: Luke 7: 36-50 
Supporting Passages: Exodus 34:6-7, Isaiah 1:18, Hebrews 10:11-28, Galatians 5

Be an Example

If we are filled with God's spirit and wisdom, why then do we hide or downplay it?
Key Activity: Who were examplary teens in the Bible, and why?
Key Passages: Matthew 5:13–16, Mark 4:21–25, Luke 8:16–18
Supporting Passages: 1 Timothy 4:12-16
