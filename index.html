ABOUT JESUS HOUSE

ONE BIG FAMILY

Jesus House Abbotsford is a parish of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) worldwide. We are a multi-ethnic Church, located in the beautiful British Columbia, passionate about God and extending His love to others. We are Raising Kingdom Champions!

WORSHIP SERVICE

Sundays, 10am
Join us for our worship service as we worship, pray, and study God's word. Yes! We have classes for children and teens. Our Pastor, David Olabode, is a man of faith and is always ready to spread God's love.

LIFE GROUPS

Weekly
We have home fellowships. Join a small group close to you, it's an opportunity to study God's word and connect with other members outside of Church. Visit us on Sunday to get connected to a life group centre.

Statement of Belief

We believe that the entire Scripture, both Old and New Testament are written by the inspiration of the Holy Spirit – II Tim. 3:16-17. All the Christian teachings and the Christian attitude of the Children of God are such as are established in the Holy Bible – II Tim. 3:10-15; II Peter 1:21; John 17:17; Ps. 119:105. Learn more...

Mission/Vison Statement

1. To make heaven.
2. To take as many people with us.
3. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ

Action Plan

To accomplish No. 1, holiness will be our lifestyle.

To accomplish No. 2 and 3, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
